SIMPLE OMELETTE RECIPE

AN easy and quick dish,perfect for meal.this classic omellete cobines beaten eggs cooked to perfect,optionally filled with your chioceof cheese,vegetable,or meats.

PREPARATION TIME

  • Total: Approximattely 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

INGREDIANTS

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fiillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Beats the eggs: a bowl,beat the egg with a pinch of salt and peeper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water and milk for a fluffier texture
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or all.
  3. Cook the omellete: the butter is melted and bubbling.pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fiillings(optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are stil runny in the middle,sprinkle your choosen fiillings over one half of the omellete.
  5. Fold and serve:As the omellete continue to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fiillings.Let it cook for another minutes,the slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
